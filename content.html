YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE

How to Take Action

Individual action, multiplied by millions of people, creates transformational change. Here's where to start.

⚡ HIGH IMPACT

Switch to Renewable Energy

Contact your energy provider to switch to a green tariff, install solar panels, or advocate for renewable energy in your community. This single change can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 1.5 tonnes CO₂/year.

⚡ HIGH IMPACT

Reduce Air Travel & Car Use

A single long-haul flight can produce more CO₂ than months of driving. Take trains, cycle, walk, or use public transit. If you must fly, consider carbon offset programs to neutralize the impact.

🔶 MEDIUM IMPACT

Shift to a Plant-Rich Diet

Meat and dairy production accounts for 14.5% of global greenhouse gas emissions. Reducing red meat consumption even one day per week significantly lowers your personal carbon footprint and reduces water usage.

🔶 MEDIUM IMPACT

Cut Plastic Use

Carry reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Avoid single-use plastics. Support brands that use sustainable packaging. Participate in local beach and river cleanups to prevent plastic from reaching the ocean.

🟢 EVERYDAY ACTION

Conserve Energy at Home

Switch to LED bulbs, unplug devices not in use, use energy-efficient appliances, and set thermostats wisely. Small changes add up efficient homes can cut energy bills by 30% and significantly reduce emissions.

🟢 EVERYDAY ACTION

Spread Awareness & Vote Green

Talk to friends and family about climate change. Share reliable information. Most importantly, vote for politicians who prioritize climate policy government action at scale is essential for meeting global climate targets.

🌱 Take the Planet Pledge

Commit to at least one action that will reduce your impact on the environment. Share it with friends and inspire others to join the movement.
